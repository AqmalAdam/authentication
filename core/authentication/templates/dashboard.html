About

KPMG in Malaysia traces its roots to 1928 and is part of a global network with a presence in 142 countries and over 275,000 professionals. With more than 2,300 staff across 8 offices in Malaysia, we bring deep industry knowledge and a collaborative approach to solving clients' challenges in Audit, Tax, and Advisory services.

We emphasize industry-specific insights, service excellence, and community impact through inclusive values and sustainable initiatives. Our leadership team is spearheaded by Managing Partner Foong Mun Kong, with strategic guidance from Non-Executive Chairman Datuk Johan Idris.
